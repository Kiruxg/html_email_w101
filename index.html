Scroll of Fortune Progress

The Scroll of Fortune events are some of the most exciting recurring events in Wizard101. Every year starting in the fall, we release the first of four scrolls that include tons of fun rewards for achieving the required points for each reward. One of the top rewards is the seasonal painting segments!
